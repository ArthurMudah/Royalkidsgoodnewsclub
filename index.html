Events

Notable Events

Our commitment to the community is reflected in events such as the Christian match we organized in December 2020, which attracted a multitude of children to join us. Additionally, our Christmas party provided an opportunity to feed and support many needy children, embodying the spirit of giving and compassion that defines our club. In addition to our past events, we have ongoing and upcoming activities that demonstrate our commitment to the community and our mission:

Annual Bible Camp

Royal Kids' Good News Club will be hosting an annual Bible camp where children will be given the opportunity to immerse themselves in biblical teachings, engage in fun activities, and form lasting friendships. This camp is a highlight of our year, fostering spiritual growth and personal development.

Community Outreach Programs

We will be actively involved in various community outreach programs aimed at making a positive impact on the lives of those in need. From providing school supplies to underprivileged children to organizing food drives, we strive to embody the teachings of Christ by serving our neighbors.

Special Guest Speakers

Periodically, we invite inspiring guest speakers to share their faith journeys and insights with our children. These experiences broaden the horizons of our young members and motivate them to pursue a deeper understanding of their Christian faith.

Talent Shows and Performances

Royal Kids' Good News Club believes in nurturing not only spiritual but also creative talents. We host talent shows and performances that allow children to showcase their abilities, whether it's in music, drama, or other artistic endeavors.
