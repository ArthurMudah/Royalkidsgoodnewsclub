History of the Club

Royal Kids' Good News Club was established in 2010 in Community 2, Tema, Italian Flat. In February 2014, we relocated to Community 3, SSNIT Flat, Block 35 A, and currently, our club meets at Mount Zion School in Community 3. Throughout our history, we have proudly raised numerous children with Christian principles, and some of our alumni have pursued careers in law and medicine.
